Two seeds were lying in the soil.

The first seed said,I want to grow! I want to send my roots deep into the soil and push my leaves through the earth to see the sunlight.”So it grew slowly and became a strong plant.

The second seed said,“I am afraid. What if my roots hit hard rocks? What if insects eat my leaves?” So it stayed in the soil and did nothing.

One day, a chicken came and ate the second seed

Moral:If we don't take chances and grow, we may lose our opportunity.
